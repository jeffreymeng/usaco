About USACO

USA Computing Olympiad

USACO (USA Computing Olympiad) is a national web based programming competition with periodic programming contests through the year. You can learn about USACO contests and their structure on the official USACO contest page.

This website is where I share my solutions to past USACO problems (either solved during an actual competition or from the USACO problem archives).
